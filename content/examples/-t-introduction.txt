# Subfolio-Astro, Running Live

Subfolio-Astro provides an elegant, practical and customizable web interface based on your file system. Point it at a folder of files and you get this — a polished, navigable
site. No CMS, no database, no page-building. **The folders below *are* the site.**

Oh yeah, free fast and secure hosting is possible through CloudFlare and Github.

Everything here is the real example content, straight out of the box.
Open any folder to see a convention at work: position embeds, enhancers,
hidden items, slideshows, thumbnails — all driven by nothing more than
how the files are named.

**Want one of your own?**\
The [Subfolio-Astro docs](https://ryanilano.github.io/subfolio-astro-docs/) walk through every convention and enhancer, start to finish.\
Source lives on [GitHub](https://github.com/ryanilano/subfolio-astro).
